[At the beginning of the interview, the subject’s eyes were closed.]

Dr. ████: I’m going to ask you a few questions. Do you think you can answer them for me?

Luna: (slowly nods)

Dr ████: All right.

[Dr. ████ organizes his papers. When he looks back at Subject Luna, her eyes are open. No irises, pupils, or whites are seen, but a blank space and what seems to be a duplicate of the milky way galaxy is present in each eye.]

[Dr. ████ is surprised and flinches.]

Dr ████: Okay. Do you know who you are?

Luna: [Indeterminate.]

Dr. ████: Can you speak up for me?

Luna: [Barely determinate.] I am in this room.

Dr. ████: I know where you are. Do you know your name?

Luna: [Clearly.] Her name isn’t to be spoken.

Dr. ████: Alright… Next question.

[The lights flicker. Dr. ████ pauses.]

Dr. ████: Did you do that?

[Luna is silent, looks up and back down at Dr. ████.]

Dr. ████: Luna, did you do that?

[The lights dim.]

Luna [Standing up.]: Do not use her name.

[As Luna speaks, the lights flicker and a few blow out, shattering the bulb. Dr. ████ shields his face. Luna sits down and the lights cease malfunctioning.]

Dr ████: [Stuttering and afraid, trying to keep a stoic demeanor.] Why don’t you want me to use your name?

Luna: Why didn’t you find her before she was turned to this? Why couldn’t you help her before she died?

Dr. ████: You are not dead, L- [He pauses, trying not to use her name.] You are not dead. You are here in this room, like you said.

Luna: I am here in this room, and she is not.

Dr. ████: Who are you?

Luna: [Indeterminate reverse speak].

Dr. ████: I said, who are you?

Luna: [Very loud indeterminate reverse speak].

Supervisor # 404: I think we need to stop. She’s getting angry.

Dr. ████: [Talking quietly Mr. Gregory ████] We’re actually getting somewhere this time, we just need to —

Supervisor #404: No, I don’t think you need to continue. We need to st-

[At this time, the Subject raised her left arm toward Dr. ████ and her left toward Supervisor #404. Both were rendered into dust-like particles of a violet and white color, disintegrated. The recording advisor quickly administered sedation chemicals into the room, calming the Subject down. He then pressed the emergency lock and rushed to the saferoom.]
